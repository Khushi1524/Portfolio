My Projects

YogaBliss

YogaBliss is a comprehensive yoga website designed to enhance your well-being. Utilizing HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP, it offers a plethora of features including a pose gallery, guided meditations, exercise routines, daily tasks, and a vibrant forum for questions and answers. Additionally, it hosts articles covering various aspects of yoga. The website is backed by a robust PHPMyAdmin database to ensure seamless functionality and user interaction.

Poetry Generator

Immerse yourself in the world of poetry with our Poetry Generator. Powered by machine learning algorithms, this project crafts unique poems based on your input words. With a beautiful user interface designed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it provides a visually captivating experience. TensorFlow and Flask work behind the scenes to generate poems dynamically, while background images set the mood for your literary journey.

Aurora

Experience the awe-inspiring beauty of the northern lights with Aurora. Tailored for enthusiasts of this natural phenomenon, the project offers a dynamic platform to explore and engage. Featuring an elegant HTML, CSS, and JavaScript-based user interface, it boasts a unique design that complements the celestial spectacle. Users can upload and showcase their photos in a dynamic gallery, engage in discussions on the community page, and share their experiences through blogs.

Text-to-voice converter

Transform text into speech effortlessly with our Text to Voice Converter. Created using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this project utilizes the SpeechSynthesis interface to convert written text into spoken words. Its simple yet effective interface makes it easy to use for a variety of applications, from assisting those with visual impairments to enhancing the accessibility of content.
